Weizi Li / 李玮孜

I am an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the University of Memphis.

Previously, I was a Hammer Fellow at MIT and I received my Ph.D. from the UNC Chapel Hill, working with Ming C. Lin. I have also spent time at Disney Research, Lenovo Research, National University of Singapore, and George Mason University.

My research interests include socio-technical systems, intelligent transportation systems, machine learning, multi-agent simulation, and robotics.
